About Us


RSS motors was founded by a group of dreamers with a

dream that would have been called pathetic a few years

ago. Their dream- electric vehicles which are low cost,

more efficient and comfortable than traditional

petrol/diesel vehicles, and all that while making

everything, down to the tyres and pipes, in India.



We at RSS motors beleive in the future of electric cars

in India. We want to establish and be the pioneers of a

new India in which people can proudly show off the name

of the brand of the product they own without having to

compromise on quality. We support the Make In India

movement and beleive in a future where every energy

that is used comes from a sustainable source and we

want to see hat future NOW. We aspire to build a new

world where our children and grandchildren can breathe

in unpolluted air and use unpolluted resources all

the while making things easier and better for the people

today. We want people to think that electric vehicles

are better in everyway and not just because it is good

for the environment
